Lego Back to the
Future Poster

This is a passion project I created purely for my own enjoyment. At the time, I had recently bought and finished the Back to the Future DeLorean Lego set and it felt like a great opportunity to show off the set and my Photoshop skills. I took the photos of the Lego Minifigures and the DeLorean in poses as close to the original poster as possible. I found stock images of a street and lightning in the sky. I remade the Back to the Future logo with the addition of Lego at the top. Once I gathered all of the individual assets, I brought them all into photoshop to create the composition seen above. While it isn’t perfect, it was very fun to create and I couldn’t be more happy with it.
