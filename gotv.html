Get Out The Vote Poster

The goal of this project was to design a set of posters for the Get Out the Vote campaign. Each poster is meant to be presented in 3 different formats, an 11x17 poster, a digital poster for screens, and a social media post. The target audience for this project was Eastern Washington University students. The concept behind this poster fell right into place. As I was searching for resources for this project, I came across rockthevote.org and the idea to use Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson as the graphic for the poster popped into my head. This worked out because a lot of people in this target audience love The Rock. For the tagline, I took a spin on one of his most famous sayings, “Do you smell what The Rock is cooking?” I had a good time designing this poster and it is another successful project.
